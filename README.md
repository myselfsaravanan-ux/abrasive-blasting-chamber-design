# 📖 Project Overview

This repository documents the design, fabrication, assembly, and commissioning of an industrial abrasive blasting chamber completed during my Research & Development Internship at **VLOG Innovations / EngineeringMonk**.

The project focused on developing a dedicated blasting chamber capable of safely containing abrasive particles during surface preparation operations while improving operator safety, workplace cleanliness, and blasting efficiency.

Through this project, I gained practical experience in engineering design, mechanical fabrication, structural assembly, workshop practices, and industrial manufacturing processes.

---

# 🎯 Project Objectives

* Design a safe and efficient abrasive blasting chamber.
* Improve containment of abrasive media during blasting operations.
* Enhance operator safety by minimizing airborne dust and particle escape.
* Develop practical skills in fabrication, assembly, and workshop engineering.
* Support industrial surface preparation processes with an integrated blasting enclosure.

---

# 🧠 Engineering Background

Abrasive blasting generates high-velocity particles and airborne dust that require controlled containment to ensure safe and efficient operation.

A blasting chamber provides a dedicated enclosed workspace where abrasive blasting can be carried out while:

* Containing abrasive particles
* Reducing dust exposure
* Improving visibility during operation
* Protecting surrounding equipment
* Supporting efficient abrasive recovery and reuse

A well-designed chamber contributes significantly to workplace safety, environmental cleanliness, and process consistency.
